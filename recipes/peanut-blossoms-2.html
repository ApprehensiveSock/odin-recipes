Ingredients

  • Lots of peanut butter
  • Lots of brown sugar
  • A few eggs
  • Some milk
  • Maybe some other stuff

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C). Grease cookie sheets.
  2. cream together the shortening, peanut butter, brown sugar, and 1 cup white sugar until smooth. Beat in the eggs one at a time, and stir in the milk and vanilla.
  3. Bake for 10 to12 minutes in the preheated oven.
  4. Eat them before they cool!
