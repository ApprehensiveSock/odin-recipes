Ingredients

  • Some hot Italian sausage
  • Some sweet Italian sausage
  • A handful of yellow diced onions
  • A lot pf dried thyme
  • Some other stuff too
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Whatever cheese is available

Steps

  1. Cut down the length of each sausage with a sharp knife. Remove and discard casings.
  2. Cook, stirring occasionally and breaking up with a spatula, until sausage begins to brown, and onion turns translucent, 5 to 7 minutes.
  3. Taste for seasoning and adjust if needed.
  4. Top with the rest of the pasta and sauce mixture and give the pan a little shake to settle the sauce. Repeat the cheese application.
